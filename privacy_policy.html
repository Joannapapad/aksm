Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 4/10/2034

Last Updated: 4/10/2024

At AKSM (referred to as "we," "us," or "our"), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, retain, and protect your personal information when you subscribe to our newsletter.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you subscribe to our newsletter:

  • Email Address: We collect your email address for the purpose of sending you our newsletter, which may contain PDF attachments with company updates, industry news, promotions, and other relevant information about our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email address to:

  • Send newsletters: We send periodic newsletters containing updates, offers, and information related to our construction services and the industry.
  • Communicate directly: We may use your email to respond to inquiries or other communications initiated by you regarding our services.

We do not use your personal information for any other purpose without your explicit consent.

3. Consent

By providing your email address and subscribing to our newsletter, you consent to receiving email communications from Aksm. You can withdraw your consent at any time by unsubscribing from our newsletter, as explained in Section 5 below.

4. Sharing of Your Information

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information with third parties except:

  • Email Service Providers: We may use third-party service providers to manage our email list and send newsletters (e.g., Google, SendGrid). These providers process your data on our behalf, and we ensure they are GDPR-compliant and implement adequate data protection measures.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or to comply with legal obligations.

5. How You Can Manage Your Information

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Unsubscribe: You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any newsletter email. Once you unsubscribe, we will remove your email address from our mailing list and cease sending you newsletters.
  • Access and Correction: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you or ask for corrections if your information is inaccurate.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at info@aksm.gr.

6. How We Protect Your Information

We are committed to ensuring that your personal data is secure. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we have implemented suitable physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect. Specifically, we use:

  • Encryption: Your email is securely transmitted and stored using encryption protocols to protect it from unauthorized access.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access the mailing list, and we restrict access based on need.

7. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you subscribe to our newsletter. If you unsubscribe, your email address will be permanently deleted from our mailing list within 30 days of your request.

8. International Data Transfers

We may use third-party email providers located outside of your home country. In such cases, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data, in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
