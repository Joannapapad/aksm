Why Choose Us

Our story starts on 2001, when Alexander Karditsas, started his own business from a 5 m2 basement in Zografou Athens. Continuously investing over the years, staying ahead of technology and innovation, today AKSM’s headquarter is based on a 460 m2 building in Mesogeion Avenue in Athens, providing a variety of turn key surveying services across the world, for developers, contractors, engineers, project managers, investors or government bodies. After 24 years our mission remains the same, to work closely with clients, fully understanding their individual needs, providing the highest standards of quality and service.

Our technical expertise and commitment to our values has implemented us on various surveying projects across the world. From conventional surveying applications to high accuracy demanding surveying services our teams have so far successfully completed more than 3000 projects, in Construction industry providing turn key surveying solutions including QA / QC services, in BIM industry providing Laser Scan, Scan2BiM and AR services, in addition to high accuracy surveying applications including deformation monitoring and reverse engineering services.

As AKSM growths over the years, today we employ more than 80 highly skilled and experienced employees, continuously training in latest surveying techniques, capable to combine technical measurement survey expertise with the latest equipment, Our teams are making the difference by providing timely, collaborative and accurate data solutions
